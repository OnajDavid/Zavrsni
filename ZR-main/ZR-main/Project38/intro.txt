=======================DON'T ESCAPE========================
Welcome to Don't Escape, a game in which You must NOT ESCAPE.
You have been cursed! This night will be a full Moon and you KNOW what that means!
You will not be able to control yourself and will SLAUGHTER everyone in your village!
To stop this, you must be swift. Act fast, Trap yourself inside. If you do not do this...
You will most certainly make the biggest mistake of your life!
=========================GOOD LUCK=========================
